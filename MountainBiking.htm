Reminders and Recommendations


  • Mountain biking can be dangerous, always wear a helment and eye protection
  • Stay on the designated trail. Shortcuts and side tracks damage fragile plant life an create erosion problems that will negatively effect the trail
  • Be courteous when coming across other trail users, especially horses ( they frighten easily)
  • Respect the trail and others, if you pack it in, pack it out. Leave the trail nicer than you found it so eveyone else can enjoy it as you have
  • Pay attention to the weather and dress appropriately
  • Take a friend or be sure to tell someone where you are going and how long you will be gone
  • Don't forget to bring water with you!
  • Know what you know and know what you don't know SO USE GOOD JUDGEMENT!
